SOUNDSCAPE NARRATIVE

A Day In The Life

 

My soundscape narrative was a project we had in class to create something with audio. I chose to make an audio version of a video log flled with sounds we hear throughout the day like classes, road noises, the shower, brushing teeth, and more. There's also background music to make it more exciting.
